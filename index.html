About Me

Evans Aboagye is HCPC registered Biomedical Scientist in the UK, and a Member of the Institute of Biomedical Science (IBMS).
A graduate from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology, Ghana, with BSc. (Hons) in Biochemistry and MPhil in Chemical Pathology.
He has been involved in several social interventions and has to his credit publications in peer reviewed journals.
He is currently a student in bootcamp as a Front-end Web developer
